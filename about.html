ABOUT ARTIST

To request an appointment, submit the Booking Form here.

Serhat Cam is a talented tattoo artist known for his unique black and white style. Specializing in abstract designs, he skillfully blends realistic images with elements that reflect his artistic vision. With a passion for creating one-of-a-kind pieces, Serhat continually explores new concepts and techniques to enhance his craft.

Based in Amsterdam, he frequently participates in tattoo conventions and guest spots, sharing his expertise and creativity with clients around the world.

To book an appointment, please fill out the Online Booking Form. For any other inquiries, feel free to reach via email.
